These are good starting settings for the extruder, i have copy pasted my entire block out of my klipper config below:

TLDR: 1/4 MS, 2766 Steps per MM, 130mm/s acceleration, low jerk (klipper doesnt use jerk), 0.28A RMS (RMS IS IMPORTANT)


# Extruder0
[extruder]
#40:1 brass gears, Full Steps
step_distance: 0.00144613156

#0.4mm Nozzle
nozzle_diameter: 0.400
filament_diameter: 1.750	
max_extrude_only_distance: 200.0
max_extrude_only_velocity: 30.0
max_extrude_only_accel: 110.0
max_extrude_cross_section: 20000.0 #this is for KISSlicer Only

[tmc2660 extruder]
microsteps: 1
interpolate: False
run_current: 0.20

#Firmware Retraction
[firmware_retraction]
retract_length: 0.5
retract_speed: 30
unretract_speed: 30
